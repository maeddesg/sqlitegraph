<objective>
Re-enable cluster overlap validation with timing-aware allocation sequencing support.

Purpose: Detect allocation corruption at runtime by validating that outgoing and incoming edge clusters do not overlap in the graph file. The original validation was disabled due to false positives from allocation timing - clusters are allocated sequentially (outgoing first, then incoming), and validation was running before both allocations completed.

Output: Runtime validation that detects genuine cluster overlap corruption without false positives during normal allocation sequencing.
</objective>
